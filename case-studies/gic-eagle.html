Enhancing Data Reliability and Delivery Efficiency in EAGLE PBOR

Organization GIC (via Zenika)
Role Product Manager
Duration Ongoing
Key Tools Jira, Datadog, SQL, Confluence, BDD

Background

The EAGLE platform serves as GIC's central Performance Book of Record (PBOR), integrating data from multiple upstream systems and providing performance analytics to investment, risk, and portfolio teams. Zenika played a key role in improving operational reliability, delivery velocity, and data integrity for this enterprise performance management platform.

PERFORMANCE ANALYTICS DATA GOVERNANCE MONITORING DEVOPS AGILE DELIVERY

The Needs

EAGLE required stronger visibility into Control-M and ETL pipelines, improved data quality across upstream/downstream flows, and more efficient collaboration between business users and developers. Increasing system complexity and unclear workflows were slowing delivery and creating operational risk.

The Process

I applied a hybrid Agile + DevOps approach, implementing:

  • Datadog dashboards for real-time monitoring of jobs, logs, and user behavior.
  • Clear Jira user stories, acceptance criteria, and automated workflow rules to streamline coordination.
  • Enhanced data tracing and validation using SQL, Splunk, and Informatica lineage.
  • Comprehensive Confluence documentation capturing specs, SIT tests, and onboarding materials.

This improved delivery predictability, observability, and data integrity across PBOR processes.

The Results

  • Faster detection and resolution of job and data issues through unified monitoring.
  • Shorter troubleshooting cycles with improved data traceability.
  • More efficient delivery via Jira automation and structured Agile practices.
  • Higher confidence in PBOR data through systematic validation and SIT testing.
  • Better onboarding and collaboration supported by consolidated documentation.
