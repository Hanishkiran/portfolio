Cybersecurity Monitoring Platform

Organization GovTech
Role Product Manager
Key Achievement 100% IM8 Compliance
Key Tools Tableau, Jira, SQL, Excel, Confluence

The Project

As Product Manager for GovTech's cybersecurity monitoring platform, I led the Agile delivery of a compliance tool that consolidated security monitoring of government ICT assets across multiple government agencies.

IM8 Compliance Asset Tracking Agile Delivery Data Visualization

The Needs

GovTech required a centralized platform for streamlined real-time monitoring across agencies by integrating monitoring of both cloud and on-premise assets. The project also involved collaborating with multiple government agencies to audit system logs and ensure 100% compliance with the IM8 policy.

The Process

I applied an Agile approach, fulfilling scrum master duties (daily stand-ups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives) to align the GCSOC team. Key activities included:

  • Backlog Management: Managing and grooming the product backlog and sprint backlog.
  • Progress Tracking: Creating visualizations like velocity and burndown charts in Jira.
  • Data Work: Utilizing SQL for data extraction and transformation, and Microsoft Excel extensively for data mapping and standardization of old and new asset information.
  • Visualization: Creating Tableau dashboards for agencies to visualize level of asset compliance to IM8 standards.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Engagement work with government agencies to ensure all asset information is captured in the compliance platform.

The Results

  • Centralization: Successful integration of cloud and on-premise assets into a centralized platform with automated capture of asset information.
  • Compliance: Achieved a 100% compliance rate with the IM8 policy across government agencies.
  • Visibility: Provided agencies with visual asset compliance reporting via Tableau dashboards.
  • Alignment: Ensured team alignment on sprint objectives and progress through effective scrum master duties.
